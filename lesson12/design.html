Site Design Elements

Color schema:

Green is strongly associated with nature, health, and organic products. Using shades of green will evoke a sense of freshness and emphasize the organic aspect of the company's products.

Vibrant Accents like yellow, orange and red will be used to add visual interest and highlight the company's energetic vibe. Using lively shades of these colors can symbolize vitality, energy, and the vibrancy of fresh produce.

Using a neutral background color, such as white or light gray, will create a clean and modern look and will allow the other colors to stand out and ensure the website remains visually appealing and easy to read.

This color scheme ensure a visually appealing website that reflects Bountiful Foods mission and will appeal to its target audience.
