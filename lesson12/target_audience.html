Audiences and Scenarios

Target audience:

The target audience for the Bountiful Foods website consists of individuals who prioritize fresh, organic, and locally sourced food options. Through research in the industry and analysis of similar websites, the following personas have been identified:

Personas:

Persona 1 -

Health-Conscious Locals: These are individuals residing in Carlsbad and the coastal areas of Los Angeles and San Diego, who value the benefits of organic and locally grown foods. They prioritize health and wellness, seeking out fresh produce and nutritious food options to support their lifestyle choices. They are likely to be environmentally conscious and interested in sustainable farming practices.

Persona 2 -

Busy Professionals: This group includes individuals with demanding work schedules who appreciate the convenience of online food ordering and delivery. They seek quick and easy access to fresh and healthy food options to maintain a balanced diet despite their busy lifestyles. These professionals are likely to value the time-saving aspect of Bountiful Foods' website and delivery services.

Persona 3 -

Tourists and Visitors: This category comprises tourists and visitors exploring the coastal areas of Carlsbad, Los Angeles, and San Diego. They are interested in experiencing the local culinary scene and are drawn to the concept of organic and locally sourced foods. These individuals are likely to rely on the website to discover the region's food offerings, learn about Bountiful Foods' food trucks, and place orders for an authentic and healthy dining experience.
