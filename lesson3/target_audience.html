Audiences and Scenarios

Target audience:

There are two different types of individuals that we think will likely be the most interested in the site. Adults that don't know alot about Vancouver and may be moving here, or visiting the area, they are looking for more information about Vancouver and it's events and attractions.

And the other individual is are business owners looking to be more involved in the community, wanting to network with other local business owners and spread the word about their own business.

Personas:

Persona 1 -

A new business owner who is looking to integrate into the local business community. Would like to reach a larger audience and offer special deals and pricing to individuals. This business owner would like to get a better feel for what make a business successful in this small community and how he can help foster a community of businesses.

Persona 2 -

This individual is moving to the pacific northwest and is looking for that city that is a perfect fit. She wants to know as much as she can about the people, events, locations, attractions and local businesses. Why would Vancouver be the place for her to move. How can it help her grow and thrive as a person and as a family.

Scenarios

  • A family that is planning to move and isn't familiar with the area.
  • User comes to the site looking for events and fun things to do while visiting Vancouver.
  • A person is wanting more information about the chamber of commerce but doesn't know how to get it.
  • Small business owner would like to be more connected to other local businesses and wants to join.
  • A member of the Chamber of Commerce looking for when is the next meeting.
